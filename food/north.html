Kadai Panner

Kadai Paneer is a popular North Indian vegetarian dish. It features cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) sautéed with bell peppers, onions, tomatoes, and a blend of aromatic spices, all cooked in a special kadai (wok) for a flavorful and slightly spicy gravy. It's a delicious and satisfying dish often served with naan or rice.

Kadai Pakora

Kadai Pakora is a deep-fried north Indian food . It consists of besan (gram flour) batter-coated vegetables like onions, potatoes, or spinach, seasoned with spices, and fried until crispy. Served with chutney, it's a popular tea-time or street food snack in India.

Rajma Chawal

Rajma Chawal is a classic North Indian dish. It features red kidney beans (rajma) cooked in a rich tomato-based gravy, seasoned with aromatic spices, and served with steamed white rice (chawal). This hearty and flavorful combination is a beloved comfort food in India.

Malpua

Malpua is a sweet north Indian desert. It's made by frying a batter of flour, milk, and sugar until golden brown, then soaked in sugar syrup. Garnished with nuts and cardamom, it's a delectable treat often enjoyed during festivals like Holi and Diwali.

Allo Gobi

Aloo Gobi is a popular Indian vegetarian dish. It combines potatoes (aloo) and cauliflower (gobi) cooked with aromatic spices, tomatoes, and onions. This flavorful curry is a staple in North Indian cuisine, served with rice or flatbreads like roti or naan.

Allo chat

Aloo Chaat is a cherished Indian street food. It comprises cubed and spiced boiled potatoes (aloo), combined with tangy tamarind chutney, yogurt, and an array of spices like chaat masala. Garnished with cilantro and sev (crispy noodles), it presents a delightful fusion of flavors and textures.
